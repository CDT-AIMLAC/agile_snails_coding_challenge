Net Energy Production
Non-cumulative net energy production after usage is removed. Negative numbers represents energy required to be brought from the grid, and positive numbers represents spare energy after usage from the building. The fainter chart corresponds to predicted values that are generated with machine-learning algorithms.
Total Energy Production
Graph displaying the power generation and consumption of the local facility. Predicted values are dashed, and are generated from a series of scientific models based of weather data and office usage patterns.
Energy Production from Wind Turbines
Graph displaying the total energy created from all wind turbines in the facility.
Predicted values are dashed and are estimated using a combination of real-world
performance curves and a theoretical wind model, alongside met office data from
the nearby weather station (Sennybridge 2).
Energy Production from Solar Panels
Total energy created from all solar panels in the facility. Predicted values are
dashed and are estimated using Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) incident on
the site panel array as a function of the time of day and includes the panel
orientation, location and the time of year. Met office data from the nearby
weather station (Sennybridge 2) is used to calculate panel temperature and
factors into panel efficiency.
